# Product Requirements Prompt (PRP) — 🔘 Pending

## 1. Project Context & Objectives
The `pitch-class-matrix` plugin currently exhibits a "viewport bleed" issue on large displays or during horizontal scrolling. The light gray background stops painting because the outermost container is strictly limited to 1024px (`max-w-5xl`). The objective is to stretch the outermost wrapper to fill the host portal's canvas entirely, relying on the inner `<main>` tag to safely constrain the content.

## 2. Technical Decisions & Architecture
* **Background Expansion:** We will strip the `max-w-5xl` and `mx-auto` utilities from the root `<div>` and inject `min-h-screen` to ensure the light background paints across the entire visible canvas (both horizontally and vertically).
* **Content Constraint Preservation:** The inner `<main className="... max-w-5xl">` tag will remain untouched, meaning the actual UI cards will not stretch or break their current layout.
