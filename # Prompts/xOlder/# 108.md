
# Product Requirements Prompt (PRP) — 🔘 Pending

## 1. Project Context & Objectives
The `StepSequencer` layout requires a visual polish pass to perfectly align with standard musical engraving and the portal's UI aesthetic.
1. The Grand Staff Brace is vertically misaligned (too high).
2. The initial barline separating the Clef column from Bar 1 is missing.
3. The chord symbols are located at the bottom of the staves and lack the "rounded pill" styling used elsewhere in the app.
The objective is to move the chord symbols to the top of the grid, wrap them in styled rounded cards, fix the SMuFL brace baseline calculation, and restore the Clef column's right border.

## 2. Technical Decisions & Dependencies
* **Target File:** `src/plugins/chord-notator/components/StepSequencer.tsx`
* **Logic Specification:**
  * **Layout Inversion:** Move the dummy spacer in the Clef Column to the top. Move the Chord Symbol container in the Sequence Map to the top. Change their heights from `h-8` to `h-10` to accommodate padding.
  * **Card Styling:** Wrap the chord text in a `<div className="bg-white shadow-sm rounded-full px-3 py-1...">` to mimic the main NotationCanvas pill.
  * **Brace Fix:** Remove `transform: translateY(-50%)` from the brace, and set its absolute top position strictly to `${MINI_STAFF * 6}px` to correctly intersect its baseline.
