# Product Requirements Prompt (PRP) — 🔘 Pending

## 1. Project Context & Objectives
The `pitch-class-matrix` plugin requires a UI overhaul to align with the application's global design system. 
1. The central grid matrix must be scaled down by 20% and moved into a discrete white card container.
2. The 88-key piano component must be miniaturized to match the exact 936px footprint of the `chord-notator` keyboard, and wrapped in its own matching white card.

## 2. Technical Decisions & Architecture
* **Matrix Scaling Math:** The current matrix is 300x325. We will scale it down to `240px` (W) by `260px` (H). This results in a perfect `20px` by `20px` grid cell dimension. SVG arrow coordinates will be updated to match.
* **Keyboard Miniaturization:** We will override the constants in `88-key.tsx` to `18px` white keys and `11px` black keys, dropping the total width from 1352px to `936px`.
* **Card Wrappers:** We will use the standard portal card classes: `bg-white border border-gray-200 rounded-lg p-3 shadow-sm`.
