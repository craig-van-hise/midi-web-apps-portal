
# Product Requirements Prompt (PRP) — 🔘 Pending

## 1. Project Context & Objectives
The `StepSequencer` "Clef Column" is visually misaligned. The staff lines are vertically lower than the rest of the 8 bars because the container lacks the bottom chord-pill spacer, altering the `50%` vertical center point. Furthermore, the Brace (`\uE000`) is misaligned, and there is an unwanted inner border separating the clef from the first bar. The objective is to restructure the Clef Column to precisely match the flex-layout height distribution of the bars, and to replicate the exact `system-left-edge` DOM structure from `NotationCanvas` for perfect Brace/Barline anchoring.

## 2. Technical Decisions & Dependencies
* **Target File:** `src/plugins/chord-notator/components/StepSequencer.tsx`
* **Logic Specification:**
  * Change the outer sequencer wrapper from `border-l-2 border-r-2 border-black` to a softer `border border-black/10` to avoid doubling up on the musical system barlines.
  * Rebuild the `Clef & Brace Column` div into a `flex flex-col` container. 
  * Add a dummy `<div className="h-8 border-t border-transparent" />` to the bottom of the Clef Column so its top `flex-1` section matches the exact height of the bars' `flex-1` section.
  * Anchor the system barline at `left-[12px]`, and anchor the Brace relative to the left of that barline.
