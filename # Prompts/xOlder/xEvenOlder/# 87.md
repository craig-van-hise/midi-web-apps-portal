# Product Requirements Prompt (PRP) — ✅ Completed

## 1. Project Context & Objectives
The current keyswitch badges are too large, resulting in visual overlap, and their solid color fills are too visually heavy. The objective is to revise the keyboard keyswitch visuals: badges must be small (~14px), white-filled with a black outline and black arrow. The semantic family color from the `TRANSFORM_SCHEMA` must instead be applied to the background of the assigned UI key itself as a 20% opacity wash, featuring a solid 2px top border.

## 2. Technical Decisions & Dependencies
* **Badge Styling:** Badges will be reduced to `14px x 14px`, `bg-white`, with a `1.5px solid black` border and black icons.
* **Key Wash Injection:** We will modify the parent key's DOM node directly to apply the background color (appending `33` to the hex for 20% alpha transparency) and the solid top border.
* **Collision Avoidance:** White key badges will be anchored at `bottom: 12px`, and black keys at `bottom: 4px`. Since white keys are 18px wide, a 14px badge guarantees a 4px gap between adjacent badges.
