# Product Requirements Prompt (PRP) — ✅ Completed

## 1. Project Context & Objectives
The `updateSpelledNotesStrip` algorithm in the `Keyboard` component fails during extreme chromatic density (e.g., a 13-note continuous cluster). The mathematical padding is too wide, and the 3-row vertical limit forces severe overlaps. The objective is to tighten the collision boundary tracking, reduce the CSS padding of the background cards, and expand the algorithm to 5 rows, allowing the 4th and 5th rows to overflow the container and elegantly hang over the top of the physical piano keys.

## 2. Technical Decisions & Dependencies
* **Target File:** `src/plugins/chord-notator/components/Keyboard.tsx`
* **Layout Adjustments:**
  * Change the `spelled-notes-strip` container class from `overflow-hidden` to `overflow-visible z-50`.
  * Expand `rowEdges` to 5 slots.
  * Define `rowPositions = ['75%', '45%', '15%', '105%', '135%']`. This prioritizes filling the grey strip first (bottom to top), and only breaches downward into the piano keys when absolutely necessary.
  * Reduce `approxWidth` multiplier to `6.0` and `PADDING` to `2`.
  * Reduce physical card padding to `0px 2px` and border-radius to `2px`.
